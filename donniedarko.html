Donnie Darko

And here we have one of my favorite movies.
The reason I enjoy this movie so much is because there is such a wonderful element of mystery present throughout the whole experience. I'm having trouble thinking of ways to describe it. It's the sort of film that you need to have wash over you. I could try to explain the plot, but that would almost ruin it. All I can say for certain is that this movie is worth watching and I found it to be an incredible experience
